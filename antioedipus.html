A Brief Collection of Thoughts Related To Anti-Oedipus, Had In the Aftermath of Reading Anti-Oedipus

On Machines

Machines do not exist in isolation as they are part of a larger network, continuously producing or interrupting flows. These machines have always been interconnected (think mouth-food, hand-tool, lungs-air). We are conglomerates of machines just as the socius is.

On Man and Nature

Expanding on that last thought, everything is a machine- a series of interconnected desiring-machines producing and interrupting flows. From this, one can correctly assume that the human body is not separate from nature. It is a series of desiring-machines that exists in a series of desiring machines, all working together and in proximity of each other, sometimes connected, these flows between machines being broken and redirected, reconfigured.

On Production

Just as man cannot be separated from nature, neither can industry. It is a false dichotomy intensified and restrutured by capitalism that man, nature, and industry are divided into separate parts- nature itself, being divided into man-nature, industry-nature, and society-nature, these constituting the basis for the differentiation of production, distribution, and consumption. However, these distinctions are neither natural nor inherent divisions, but ones shaped and constructed by and under a capitalist system.

Production, distribution, and consumption are three ways of viewing the same simultaneous process- everything is production, is distribution, is consumption; without mediation. It is a form of delirium to try and separate these into independent spheres or circuits.

On being faced with the uncodable…

1.The repressive apparatus puts itself into motion: “if we cant code it, we will try to annihilate it.” (When I hear desire, I go for my gun.) If it cant eliminate it…

2. We try to find new axioms in which these flows exist (for better or for worse, but nonetheless exist) in a form that is operable within the socius in its current state. These axioms can shift as the flow shifts.

On axioms:

While early socii code flows through established structures, capitalism operates by decoding the flows and re-integrating them through axioms, which are flexible and adjustable.
