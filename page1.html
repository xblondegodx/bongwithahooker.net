funny thing

one of the funniest things that can happen to you is when you realize that those songs- the ones about having your heart broken or having some wrong done to you by a lover- are not about you, but about things you’ve actually done to other people.

on my run today, i was about halfway through when the song WASTED by tiberius b came on. the beginning of the song starts like this-

“tell me how good was the fuck the one you chose over us did the freedom make it worth your time?”

the first few times i heard this song i remember thinking, yeah. yeah did it? well? to all my exes. eventually though i had the thought- how many times did this actually happen to me? how many times was i faced with the awful realization that someone i was dating chose a fling over what we had?

and i quickly realized that number was maybe 1. at most. and i don’t even think that’s the case. so in all fairness to that person and in all honesty to myself, i will say that number is 0. in countenance, how many times have i done that? the number is more than 0.

this probably makes me sound like a huge asshole and granted it should. i think for a long time i was a huge asshole. it’s pretty remarkable to think that until maybe last year, the end of last year no less, was when i really had the realization that i was the problem. truly something was broken inside of me that i tried to fix through failed attempts at therapy along with drugs, failed attempts at relationships along with drugs, and in general many failed attempts (with drugs). obviously none of that worked.

it’s only been a few months since then and i will say some part of me seems to have healed. i emphasize some part because in no way is it done. that being said, i spent 3 months in a happy relationship before things got bad and at no point did i fuck things up. well, at no point did i choose a fuck over us. which was something. of course this did end, and i did end things, but it was not how most of these things end for me, which again, was something.

it ended when she wouldn’t stop yelling at me and hitting me. and when i broke up with her, she hit me a lot, but that’s fine. i felt like some part of me deserved that, you know? and i mean, some part of me did deserve that. a lot of me wishes i didn’t end things over a small amount of what some people would call abuse. she was a beautiful girl who loved very passionately, and i can respect that. i love that.

anyway, it’s just kind of a funny thing. if you have read on this far that’s great. thanks.
